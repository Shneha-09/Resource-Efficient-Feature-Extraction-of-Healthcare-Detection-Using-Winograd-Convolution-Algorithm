💚 Normal

A steady and healthy heartbeat with consistent waveforms and no abnormal electrical activity.

⚠️ Abnormal Heartbeat

Shows signs of arrhythmias irregular rhythms such as skipped or extra beats. These may suggest underlying electrical issues.

🕰️ History of Myocardial Infarction

Evidence of a past heart attack. These ECG traces reflect scar tissue or damaged areas from prior cardiac events.

🚨 Myocardial Infarction

Suggests an ongoing or very recent heart attack. The ECG reflects acute abnormalities needing urgent medical evaluation.
